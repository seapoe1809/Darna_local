CONNECT YOUR SERVER

1. Goto Nextcloud app if installed.

2. Connect NextCloud to iOS Files.

3. Connect Nextcloud to MacOS.

Connect NextCloud to Files in iOS

This guide allows you to connect your Nextcloud to 'Files' in iOS. Downloading the app and configuring your app will allow it to automatically connect to files. However the steps to access it are as follows if you would still like to access it from "Files":

1. Open "Files" on your iOS.

2. Click on "Browse". THis the last tab to the bottom right of the screen. Click "Browse" again until you arrive at "Browse" menu. It will have several options on your screen.

3. The subheadings include "Locations", "Favorites", "Shared". "Nextcloud" might show show up in "Locations" if the app is already configured. In this case you are done and could connect by clicking on "Nextcloud".
